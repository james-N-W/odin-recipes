Savor the Simplicity of Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Roman pasta dish known for its creamy texture
and bold, savory flavors. It requires just a handful of ingredients but delivers an
indulgent, restaurant-quality meal in minutes. Unlike cream-based pasta sauces,
carbonara relies on eggs and cheese to create its rich, velvety consistency,
enhanced by crispy pancetta or bacon. With each twirl of pasta, you experience
the harmony of salty, smoky, and cheesy goodness.

Quick and Delicious Carbonara at Home

This dish comes together quickly, making it a great choice for busy weeknights
or impromptu dinners. Simply cook your pasta, crisp the pancetta, and mix it all
together with a silky sauce made from eggs, cheese, and pepper. The heat from
the pasta cooks the eggs into a luscious sauce without scrambling them.
Top with extra cheese and a sprinkle of black pepper, and dinner is served!

Ingredients

  • Parmesan cheese or Pecorino Romano, grated
  • Black pepper
  • Parsley
  • Pancetta or bacon, diced
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Spaghetti (or your favorite pasta)
  2. Salt (for boiling pasta)
  3. Pancetta or bacon, diced
  4. Garlic, minced (optional)
  5. Eggs
  6. Parmesan cheese or Pecorino Romano, grated
  7. Black pepper, freshly ground
  8. Parsley (for garnish, optional)
